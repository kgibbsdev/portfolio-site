About

Golden Treasure

"Golden Treasure is a game for those who carry dragons in their hearts. Hunt, explore, survive, and become Great among the True Children of Sun and Earth." - Golden Treasure Steam Page

I was contracted by Dreaming Door Studios to help fix bugs in their hit title Golden Treasure as well as create a tool to aid in the translation of the game into other languages. I collaborated with their team and the Golden Treasure community to deliver the highest quality experience possible.

Technologies Used: Unity, C#, XML, Proprietary Story Creation Tooling

Wanderbot

Protect your robot friend from bombs raining down from the sky! Think fast as he wanders into powers that can help or hurt your cause!

Wanderbot is my first shipped title. Created over the summer through a collaboration with a local artist and a local musician. The game released in September of 2017 on itch.io.

Technologies Used: Gamemaker Studio, GML

Wendell's Extreme Feeding Contest

Wendell used to be the blood-drinking champion of the world, but in recent years he's lost his touch. Does he still have what it takes to chug with the best of them? Compete against two rival to reclaim your former glory!

Wendell's Extreme Feeding Contest is a game jam game that I made with a fellow programmer and designer Kazmuir Long with the theme one chance.

Technologies Used: Unity, C#
